16 Family-Favorite Ground Beef Casseroles

These ground beef casseroles offer quick-and-easy comfort food that everyone in your family will love. Spaghetti Casserole Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Cream Cheese
  • Cheeses
  • Oil
  • Sirloin
  • Onion
  • Seasonings
  • Sauce

How to Make Spaghetti Casserole

  1. Boil and drain the spaghetti.
  2. Cook the beef until lightly browned, then add onion and seasonings.
  3. Continue cooking until the meat is cooked through, then drain the grease.
  4. Stir the sauce into the beef mixture, then add the spaghetti and stir until coated in sauce.
  5. Assemble the spaghetti casserole according to the detailed instructions.
  6. Bake, covered with foil, for 30 minutes.
  7. Remove the foil, sprinkle with Parmesan, and broil until the cheese is golden brown.
